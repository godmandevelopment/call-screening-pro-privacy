Call Screening Pro - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Privacy Summary: Call Screening Pro respects your privacy by processing everything locally on your device and never collecting, storing, or sharing any personal information.

Overview

Call Screening Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information when you use our call screening services.

Data Collection and Usage

What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted
  • No call logs are saved or shared
  • No contact information is accessed or stored
  • No location data is collected
  • No usage analytics are tracked
  • No advertising data is gathered

Local Processing Only

  • All call screening happens locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud services
  • Your privacy is completely protected

Permissions Explained

READ_PHONE_STATE Permission

  • Purpose: Required to identify incoming phone calls for screening
  • Usage: Only used to detect incoming calls and apply your blocking rules
  • Data Handling: Phone state information is processed locally and never stored
  • No Data Storage: No phone numbers or call information is saved

Other Permissions

  • CALL_SCREENING_SERVICE: Allows the app to screen incoming calls
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Enables the app to run call screening in the background
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Allows notifications when calls are blocked

Data Security

  • Local Processing: All operations happen on your device
  • No Network Communication: The app does not connect to the internet for call screening
  • Secure Storage: Any app settings are stored securely on your device only

Third-Party Services

Call Screening Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all processing
  • No data needs to be deleted as none is collected or stored
  • Your privacy is fully under your control
